Gluten Free Bread

Mom

    Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups all purpose gluten free flour (Bob's Red Mill All Purpose Gluten Free Baking Flour)
  • 1 tsp xanthum gum
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 1 pkg rapid rise yeast (2.25 tsp)
  • 0.25 cup honey
  • 1 tsp apple cider
  • 1.5 cup warm water (100-110 deg Fahrenheit)
  • 3 egg whites (large eggs) at room temperature
  • 1 tsp salt

    Steps

  1. Spray a 9x5in bread pan with baking spray OR a 9x4in Pullman Loaf pan
  2. Move top oven rack to middle position
  3. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Add gluten-free flour, baking powder, and instant yeast to a large bowel - Stir to combine all ingredients
  5. Add olive oil, honey, apple cider vinegar, egg whites, and warm water to flour mixture - Mix on low for 1 minute *do not use dough hook* (This mixture will look like thick cake batter)
  6. Pour dough into sprayed pan
  7. Cover with sprayed plastic wrap
  8. Cover pan with kitchen towel and allow bread to rise in a warm place for 30 minutes (do not allow bread to rise longer than 30 minutes)
  9. Place in the oven to bake for 30 to 45 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean (bread will turn golden brown - internal temperature will be between 205 to 210 degrees Fahrenheit)
  10. Allow the loaf to cool in pan for 10 minutes, then remove the loaf from the baking pan and place on a wire rack to cool (if bread completely cools in the pan, the steam can get trapped and the loaf can get soggy - especially the bottom)
  11. Allow the bread to cool completely before slicing
